Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vegetables do you sell

We offer a wide variety of fresh, locally sourced vegetables including leafy greens like spinach and lettuce, root vegetables such as potatoes and carrots, and seasonal items like tomatoes, cucumbers, and peppers. We ensure that all our produce is fresh and of the highest quality.

What are your working hours?

Our store is open every day from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. You can place orders online anytime, and we will process them during our regular business hours.

Do you offer home delivery for vegetables?

Yes, we offer home delivery services within a specified area. You can place an order through our website or contact us directly

Are the vegetables organic

We offer both organic and conventional vegetables. Our organic vegetables are grown without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.
